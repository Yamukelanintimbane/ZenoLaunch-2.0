About ZenoLaunch

Empowering digital transformation in South Africa

The Company

ZenoLaunch is a Durban-based digital solutions provider founded by Yamukelani Ntimbane. We specialize in creating responsive web applications and e-commerce platforms tailored for the South African market.

Since our inception, we've helped over 50 local businesses establish their digital presence, with solutions that increased mobile conversions by 35% on average.

Founder & Lead Developer

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality digital solutions for South African entrepreneurs by providing:

  • Affordable, locally-relevant tech solutions
  • Education-first development approach
  • Mobile-optimized platforms
  • Ongoing technical support

We believe every business deserves a digital presence that reflects their vision.

Our Vision

To become the leading catalyst for digital transformation in KwaZulu-Natal by 2025, focusing on:

1

Bridging the tech gap for township businesses

2

Developing SA-specific e-commerce solutions

3

Creating 100 tech jobs in Durban
