About The Developer


I'm Yamukelani Ntimbane, a passionate Front-End Developer & Entrepreneur from Durban, South Africa. My journey in tech began with a fascination for how digital interfaces could solve real-world problems, particularly in the South African context.

As the Founder of ZenoLaunch and technical lead for ventures like LuxAura Scents, I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly web applications that deliver real business value. My work has included building e-commerce platforms that increased mobile conversions by 35% and developing educational resources for under-resourced schools that served over 5,000 monthly users.

"Every line of code should solve a problem or enhance someone's experience. That's development with purpose." This philosophy drives my approach to front-end development, where I combine technical expertise in React, JavaScript, and UI/UX design with a deep understanding of local market needs.

Through ZenoLaunch, I'm now focused on empowering other entrepreneurs with the technical tools to bring their visions to life. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring the intersection of AI and front-end development or mentoring upcoming developers in Durban's growing tech scene.
